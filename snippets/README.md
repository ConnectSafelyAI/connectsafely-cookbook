## What belongs here vs `skills/`

- Snippet → one endpoint, no orchestration. "Send a message", "fetch a profile", "search people for keyword".
- Skill → multi-step output that solves a job. "Research a prospect", "draft a comment", "score an inbound lead".

If your snippet grows past ~80 lines or chains multiple endpoints, promote it to a skill.
